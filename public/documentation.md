# Spinal Anaesthetic

## Consent

**Common (1:10 to 1:100)**

-   Low blood pressure
-   Nausea and Vomiting
-   Itching
-   Urinary retention and urinary catheterisation
-   Pain during injection
-   Headache
-   Significant post-dural puncture headache (1:200)

**Rare (1:10,000)**

-   Nerve Damage causing loss of sensation and weakness, normally lasting days to weeks

**Very Rare (1:50,000)**

-   Permanent Nerve Damage
